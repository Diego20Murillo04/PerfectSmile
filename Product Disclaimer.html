Caution


Keep out of reach of young children. All Perfect Smile Experience LLC products are not intended for use in children under the age of 12. Do not swallow impression material, or swallow any volume of teeth whitening gel or desensitizing gel beyond the amount consumed during general use (see instructions). Avoid eye contact. Rinse eyes thoroughly with water if gel comes in contact with them. Handle gel with dry hands. Gel that comes into contact with the skin can cause temporary skin irritation and/or whitening. Wash hands with soap and water after applying the gel. If large volume of teeth whitening gel or tooth desensitizing gel is consumed, contact poison control immediately.
